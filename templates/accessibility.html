Accessibility Hub

Every fan deserves an amazing World Cup experience. We're here to help.

♿

Wheelchair Access

All 16 venues are fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, elevators, and dedicated seating areas with companion seats and clear sightlines.

👁️

Visual Assistance

Audio description services, high-contrast mode, screen reader compatibility, and Braille signage at all key locations.

👂

Hearing Support

Hearing loop systems in all seating areas, sign language interpreters at information desks, and visual alert systems.

🧠

Sensory Rooms

Quiet, sound-dampened spaces available at every venue for fans who need a sensory break from the excitement.

🐕‍🦺

Service Animals

Service animals are welcome at all venues. Relief areas and water stations are available throughout each stadium.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Services

Nursing rooms, stroller parking, children's play areas, and family-friendly viewing sections at every venue.
